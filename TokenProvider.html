Interface: TokenProvider

TokenProvider

A TokenProvider represents a source for token metadata. It can be any possible source, though generally speaking it would be an indexer of blockchain/marketplace/contract transaction metadata. A TokenProvider can have its own set of constructor arguments, and may provide additional methods.
